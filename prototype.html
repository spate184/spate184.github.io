Alpine Coaster Development at Veterans Boulevard

Proposed commercial development for an Alpine Coaster attraction located off Veterans Boulevard in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee. The property is on a hill and contains an existing stream.

Course

Senior Design Project Course Sequence: CE399S & CE400.

Goal

Provide engineering plans/calculations to support permitting and construction (as a student deliverable package).

Project Scope

Major discipline responsibilities summarized from the drafted scope of work.

Transportation

  • Traffic Impact Study; analyze access points and off-site improvements (turn lanes, etc.).
  • Design entry driveways; determine parking count and coordinate with site civil.
  • Fire truck + garbage truck turning movements for compliance.

Site Civil / Stormwater / Utilities

  • Site layout + grading coordination.
  • Entrance road + profile.
  • Hydrology + stormwater detention; conveyance sizing.
  • Road stream crossing + hydraulic calcs.
  • Water (domestic + fire) and sanitary sewer design + hydraulic calcs.

Construction / Environmental

  • Project schedule + cost estimate.
  • Site-specific SWPPP and ARAP per TDEC requirements.

Geotechnical

  • Geotech calculations for retaining walls as needed (driveways, lots, stream crossing, and/or coaster structure).
